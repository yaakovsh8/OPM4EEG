File Description:
This file lists EEG signal patterns identified during a comparison between the ADHD and CONTROL groups. Each entry describes a unique 5-label pattern observed on a specific EEG channel, showing a significant difference in occurrence between the two groups. Only patterns with a percentage gap (GAP) of at least 10% are included, which may suggest group-specific characteristics.

 How to read:
- Group A – The group where the pattern is primarily observed
- Group B – The comparison group
- GAP – The absolute difference in occurrence rates between the two groups
- Channel – EEG channel number where the pattern was found
- Labels – The sequence of EEG frequency band labels (e.g., δ, θ, α)
